1 Week

Team

C#

Project Details

This was a team submission for the Plymouth game jam 2020 with the theme ‘Untouchable’, developed by a team of 4. This included 2 programmers, 1 designer and 1 artist and took place over 1 week in the summer of 2020.

Main Menu

One area I worked on for tumble town was the main menu in which we used world space UI.

Object pooling

For a university worksheet we were tasked with optimising a previous project we had worked on, since tumble town was a game jam it was likely that it wasn’t well optimised. One of the changes I made for this was an object pool which would hold a set number of bullets to be used by the enemies rather than having them instantiated when they are needed.

What Went well

Overall, I am happy with how tumble town turned out and how much we produced in the space of a week.

An area I am happy with is the object pooler whilst in the grand scheme of things it didn’t affect the performance that much it was something new that I learned and is something that I can use for future projects where there may be a lot more objects that get instantiated.

What Could be improved

An area I would liked to have improved on is the AI movement as the original idea was to just have stationary enemies before we added ones which would chase the player meaning they had no path navigation and just moved in the direction of the player.
