About

This is the game me and 3 others made for the Plymouth game jam 2020 which lasted a week. With the theme was untouchable.

Wanted for your unforgivable crimes against the Cactus people, you, a lonesome tumbleweed - one of the last of your kind never stops tumblin'

Escape from each town, running away from the Cactus Cops as you go from town to town; hoping to escape the wild wild west.
